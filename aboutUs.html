We're Melbourne Reboot. We want to encourage our users to get together and reconnect with the best of Melbourne after much time apart. Whether you want to get your coffee fix, explore a special neighburhood we can help you get motivated and inspired to support local communities through the app.

Simply select what you would like to do today, set your distance and choose from your top results, you can even create a list of favourites to explore later on. Just so you can prepare you all four seasons in Melbourne, we've also provided you with a easy weather report just so you know which season to pack for the day!

There's places you've missed, people you haven't visited in a while, and scenes you've wanted to get back into. Jump on our app reboot your support for Melbourne.
